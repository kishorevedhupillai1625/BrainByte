INTRODUCTION :

In today’s fast-paced academic world, students and educators face the challenge of processing large volumes of information in a limited amount of time. While book summarizing tools and MCQ generators exist independently, there is no widely available platform that combines both into a single, seamless AI-powered solution. Our project, Brain Byte, bridges this gap by providing an intelligent system that can read and summarize entire chapters, highlight key concepts, and automatically generate multiple-choice questions based on the content. This unique integration not only saves time but also enhances learning efficiency by enabling instant revision and self-assessment. By incorporating advanced features such as keyword extraction, difficulty-level customization for MCQs, and multiple exam modes, our solution offers a personalized and engaging learning experience.

ABSTRACT:

This project uses AI to convert PDF books into concise chapter summaries and exam-focused MCQs. Built with Python, PDFMiner, and NLP, it saves students’ time by automating note-taking and question creation, making learning more efficient and practical.

IMPORTANCE:

  • Saves time in note-making
  • Easy exam preparation
  • Reduces workload for teachers
  • Improves learning efficiency
